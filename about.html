About

Mina Mehdizadeh is an architect and instructor with teaching experience at Technical and Vocational University of Kerman (2025–present), School of Art in Kerman, and University of Allame Jafari in Rafsanjan (2022–2025). She has also worked as a residential plan designer at City Office25 Company (2020–2022).

She earned her Master of Architecture (GPA 16.27/20) from Shahid Bahonar University of Kerman (2018–2020), where her thesis focused on designing an Autism Center with an emphasis on communication with the environment and people. She completed her Bachelor of Architecture (GPA 15.33/20) at Azad Islamic University of Kerman (2013–2017), with a thesis on designing a Carpet Museum inspired by the flexibility of hand-woven carpets.

Her research interests include sustainable energy building, green building materials, smart building technologies, energy efficiency, and sustainable architecture.
