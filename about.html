About

Mina Mehdizadeh is an architect and university instructor. She holds an M.Arch from Shahid Bahonar University of Kerman and a B.Arch from Islamic Azad University of Kerman. Her experience includes residential plan design and teaching architectural design and AutoCAD. Interests: sustainable architecture, smart building technologies, green materials.

  • M.Arch (2018–2020) — Shahid Bahonar University of Kerman
  • B.Arch (2013–2017) — Islamic Azad University of Kerman
  • Instructor — Faculty of Architecture (2023–present)
